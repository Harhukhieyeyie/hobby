Beyond Books

Reading naturally led me into related interests such as history, design, and material culture. Books often serve as gateways — prompting questions about how societies function, how ideas evolve, and how meaning is preserved over time.

For me, reading is not about quantity, but engagement. It is a deliberate practice of slowing down, thinking carefully, and allowing ideas to mature rather than rushing to conclusions.

“Turning pages, expanding minds.”
